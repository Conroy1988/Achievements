## v1.4.0 — Evidence quality

Released **20 July 2026**.

## Added

- Event-linked evidence records that preserve negative, delayed, and inconclusive outcomes.
- Threshold-boundary investigations with explicit brackets, blockers, and completion criteria.
- Fail-closed evidence adjudication rules, contradiction assessments, and deterministic release-readiness gates.
- Evidence-intelligence pressure scoring and seven bounded acquisition missions.
- Mission-specific intake, privacy screening, human review, conflict disclosure, and controlled dispositions.
- Non-executable promotion plans with exact impact previews, validation requirements, and rollback scope.
- Six-record public reconstruction corpus for Quickdraw, YOLO, and Pair Extraordinaire.
- GitHub-owned live achievement-fragment corpus with exact criteria, linked history, and normalized fingerprints.
- Complete first-party milestone histories for Pull Shark, Galaxy Brain, and Starstruck.
- Weekly live-fragment drift verification.
- Static API discovery expanded to forty public JSON files.

## Changed

- Evidence coverage increased from **44.2** at the v1.3.0 baseline to **91.2/100 — Robust**.
- Twelve of thirteen canonical claims are now official or confirmed.
- Only Pair Extraordinaire numerical tier thresholds remain below confirmed.
- Quickdraw's five-minute timing contract is now official.
- Pull Shark milestones are now officially documented as **2, 16, 128, and 1,024** merged pull requests.
- Galaxy Brain milestones are now officially documented as **2, 8, 16, and 32** accepted answers.
- Starstruck milestones are now officially documented as **16, 128, 512, and 4,096** repository stars.
- YOLO's broad contract is now officially documented as **Merged without a review**.
- Pair Extraordinaire's broad contract is now officially documented as **coauthored commits on merged pull requests**.
- Open contradictions reduced from six to three.

## Corrected

- Replaced community-only milestone classifications where GitHub's live product fragments publish the exact contracts.
- Separated broad trigger certainty from unresolved edge cases, processing behaviour, transfer semantics, and attribution rewrites.
- Removed stale blocked-release wording after the merged-main evidence and operational gates passed.

## Known limitations

- Pair Extraordinaire numerical tier thresholds remain community-reported because GitHub's x4 detail fragment does not publish the counts.
- YOLO alternate review states and structured merger identity remain unresolved edge cases.
- Pair Extraordinaire squash, rebase, email-mismatch, and rewritten-history behaviour remains unresolved.
- Starstruck repository transfer, organisation ownership, forks, archival, falling counts, and persistence remain unresolved.
- Achievement processing and profile visibility may still be delayed independently of a qualifying event.
- Public product fragments can change; the weekly monitor fails closed on criterion or fingerprint drift.
