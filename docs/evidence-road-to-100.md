## Score milestones

- **54.6:** official-source reconciliation complete.
- **70.0+:** promote the most reproducible active triggers and close at least two critical disputes.
- **85.0+:** confirm every trigger and most tier boundaries through independent evidence.
- **100.0:** every claim is official and every contradiction is resolved. This requires GitHub to publish the remaining private contract details; independent reproduction alone can raise claims to confirmed, which scores 85 rather than 100.

## Integrity rule

A claim is promoted only when its acceptance criteria are met and the supporting evidence survives privacy, attribution, date, contradiction, and independent-review checks. Negative results are retained rather than discarded.
