# Maintenance Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing the reference project.

- Re-check active and retired achievement status.
- Confirm community-observed thresholds against multiple current sources.
- Verify that every guide states its evidence level.
- Check internal links and file names.
- Review issue and pull-request templates for accuracy.
- Record material behavioural changes in the changelog.
- Remove stale claims rather than silently preserving them.
- Keep examples legitimate and avoid encouraging spam or fabricated engagement.

A full maintenance review should record the review date and the reviewer in the pull-request description.
