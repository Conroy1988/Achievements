# Verification failure reasons

A test may be inconclusive or fail because:

- the action was attributed to a different account;
- the qualifying event did not complete;
- repository visibility or ownership differed from the expected conditions;
- the profile had not finished processing;
- the evidence omitted essential timestamps or links;
- the tested behaviour had changed;
- the observation could not be reproduced.

Record the specific reason rather than labelling every missing badge as a platform failure.
