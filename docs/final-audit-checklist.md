# Final Documentation Audit Checklist

Use this checklist before declaring a major documentation milestone complete.

- All intended changes are merged and attributed correctly.
- Files are substantive, uniquely scoped, and indexed.
- Claims include current status, evidence, dates, and limitations.
- Links, privacy controls, and archive references are valid.
- Open disputes and maintenance owners are recorded.
- The final merged pull-request count has been independently verified.
