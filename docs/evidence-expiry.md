# Evidence expiry

Evidence does not automatically become false with age, but its ability to support a current platform claim decreases over time.

Review evidence when:

- the interface or feature has changed;
- the source is no longer accessible;
- the observation predates a known platform update;
- newer reproducible evidence conflicts with it;
- the claim is expressed as current rather than historical.

Mark older evidence as historical when it remains useful but no longer establishes present behaviour.
