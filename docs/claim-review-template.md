# Claim Review Template

## Claim

State the exact achievement behaviour or numerical threshold being reviewed.

## Current classification

Official, reproduced, community observed, needs verification, retired, or unavailable.

## Evidence reviewed

List current official sources, independent references, and reproduction records with dates.

## Conflicting evidence

Describe material disagreement or environmental differences.

## Conclusion

State whether the claim is confirmed, corrected, narrowed, deprecated, or left unresolved.

## Follow-up

Identify any controlled test, documentation update, or future review required.
