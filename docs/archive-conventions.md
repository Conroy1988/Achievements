# Archive conventions

Historical achievement material should remain distinguishable from current guidance.

- Add an archive notice at the top of the document.
- State why the material is no longer current.
- Record the date it was superseded or retired.
- Link to the replacement guidance where available.
- Preserve source links and historical context.
- Remove archived material from current-action checklists.

Archiving is preferred to silent deletion when the information remains useful for research.
