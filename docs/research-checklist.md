# Research Checklist

Before adding or changing an achievement claim:

- Search current official GitHub documentation and changelogs.
- Compare at least two independent community references for undocumented thresholds.
- Check publication and verification dates.
- Separate the triggering behaviour from tier progression counts.
- Identify whether repository visibility, account ownership, reviews, or other settings may affect the result.
- Record contradictory evidence rather than selecting only the preferred source.
- Label inferences clearly.
- Add a last-verified date to the resulting guide.

Research quality is determined by traceability and accurate uncertainty, not by the number of links collected.
