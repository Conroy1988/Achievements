# Code of Conduct

Contributors must communicate respectfully, evaluate claims on evidence, and avoid harassment, impersonation, manipulation, or coordinated abuse.

## Expected behaviour

- Challenge information without attacking individuals.
- Correct mistakes transparently.
- Respect privacy and consent.
- Avoid spam, fabricated engagement, fake identities, and deceptive achievement farming.
- Keep discussions relevant to GitHub achievement documentation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive content, close disruptive contributions, and restrict participation where necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
