## Contribute

Contributions are welcome when they improve accuracy, clarity, accessibility, or reproducibility.

## Good contributions

- Correct an outdated or unsupported claim
- Add dated evidence for an achievement trigger or tier
- Improve an achievement guide with clearer steps and caveats
- Document a failed reproduction or platform change
- Fix broken links, navigation, accessibility, or terminology
- Add privacy-safe screenshots or examples

## Required evidence

For behavioural or threshold claims, provide:

1. The achievement and exact claim being tested
2. The date and relevant GitHub account context
3. The qualifying action or reproduction steps
4. Public URLs where possible
5. Screenshots only when they add information not visible in the URL
6. Known limitations, alternative explanations, and uncertainty

## Contribution process

1. Search existing issues and guides for overlapping work.
2. Open an issue when the change involves a disputed or substantial claim.
3. Create a focused branch and keep the change narrowly scoped.
4. Explain the evidence status in the pull request.
5. Respond to review questions and revise unsupported wording.

## Integrity rules

Do not submit fabricated evidence, empty activity, misleading attribution, private personal data, or instructions that encourage platform abuse. Community reports must be labelled as such until independently reproduced.

## Writing style

Use direct headings, concise paragraphs, descriptive links, accessible tables, and explicit evidence labels. Separate confirmed facts from inference and community observation.
